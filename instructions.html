Instructions:

  • In this game, there are 3 subject. The player has to choose one of them.
  • Each subject will have 15 questions having 5 points each.
  • Player will have 30 Seconds for the questions 1-5.
  • Player will have 40 Seconds for the questions 5-10.
  • Player will have 50 Seconds for the questions 11-15.
